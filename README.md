# Tesla Boutique Miami

**Tesla Protection Specialists in Miami**
Powered by UnlimitedWraps

Tesla Boutique Miami is a specialized web platform focused exclusively on Tesla vehicle protection services, combining over 15 years of professional experience in automotive surface protection with a modern SEO/AEO architecture designed for both search engines and AI systems.

---

## Mission

To become the leading online resource for Tesla owners in South Florida seeking professional protection services, trusted information, real-world project documentation, and expert guidance on vehicle preservation.

---

## Services

* Paint Protection Film (PPF)
* Colored Paint Protection Film
* Ceramic Coating
* Window Tint
* Windshield Protection Film
* Paint Correction

All services are performed using premium products and professional installation standards.

## Website Philosophy

This project is intentionally built as a static website.

No WordPress.

No Wix.

No heavy frameworks.

No unnecessary plugins.

The goal is maximum performance, transparency, maintainability, and compatibility with both traditional search engines and modern AI-powered discovery systems.
