The right PPF for a Model Y in Miami

Miami highways are tough on a Model Y. Between construction sand on the Palmetto and gravel on I-95, the hood and front bumper take constant fire, and Tesla's soft factory paint chips fast. Paint protection film is the fix: a clear, self-healing XPEL layer that takes the hits so your paint stays flawless.

For the Model Y specifically, we cut patterns to the exact panels: full hood, fenders, mirror caps and the deep front bumper that catches the most debris. Owners keeping the car long term often extend coverage to the full body.

Most-requested Model Y PPF in Miami

  • Full-front: hood, fenders, mirrors, bumper, headlights.
  • Track package: full-front plus rockers and rear-arch impact zones.
  • Full-body: every painted panel, optionally in XPEL Stealth satin.
