Protecting the Cybertruck in South Florida

The Cybertruck is unlike any other Tesla because its body is bare stainless steel, not painted. That changes everything: there is no clear coat to chip, but the stainless scratches, smudges and shows fine marks. Paint protection film over the stainless keeps it pristine and far easier to clean, while colored PPF is the way to give a Cybertruck real color with full protection underneath.

We film the Cybertruck's large flat panels with genuine XPEL, including satin and stealth options that suit its angular design.
