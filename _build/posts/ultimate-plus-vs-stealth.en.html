First, the part that should relax you: the protection is the same

ULTIMATE PLUS and STEALTH are both genuine XPEL paint protection films, built on the same technology. Both guard against rock chips, road debris, bug acids and UV. Both have the self-healing top coat that makes light scratches and swirl marks disappear with heat (and in Miami, the sun does that job for free). Both carry XPEL's 10-year warranty. So this is not a choice between more or less protection. It is a choice between two looks.

ULTIMATE PLUS: the invisible one

ULTIMATE PLUS is XPEL's flagship high-gloss clear film. Installed properly, it is virtually undetectable: your Tesla keeps its exact factory color, depth and shine, including metallic and pearl finishes. If you love how your Pearl White or Ultra Red looks in the showroom and want it to look exactly like that in five years, this is your film.

Because it is invisible, it also gives you flexibility in coverage. A full front package (bumper, hood, fenders, mirrors) blends seamlessly with the rest of the car, so you can protect the impact zone without committing to full body.

STEALTH: the transformation

STEALTH is the same protection with a satin finish. Installed over glossy factory paint, it converts the whole look of the car to a smooth, uniform satin: think satin black Model Y, satin Midnight Silver Model 3. It is the factory matte look Tesla does not sell, and it turns heads everywhere.

Two things make STEALTH special. First, it is fully reversible: remove the film and your original gloss paint is underneath, untouched. Second, if you ever own a car with factory matte paint, STEALTH is the only correct film for it, because it preserves the matte texture instead of adding gloss. It also sits next to our colored PPF options if you want a true color change rather than a satin of your current color.

One honest note on coverage: because STEALTH changes the appearance, it does not blend with unprotected gloss panels the way ULTIMATE PLUS does. For the satin conversion to look right, we recommend covering the complete car. That makes it a bigger project than a gloss full front, and it is worth knowing before you fall in love with the look.

Living with each finish

Day to day, both films are easy to live with. Wash with a pH-neutral soap, skip brush-style automatic car washes, and dry the car instead of letting water spots bake in the sun (mineral water spots are the one thing no film warranty covers). The practical differences: gloss shows fingerprints and water spots more, but admits all the classic detailing rituals. Satin quietly hides minor imperfections, but never gets waxed or polished, because anything that adds gloss works against the finish. Either film can be topped with XPEL FUSION PLUS ceramic to make washing even easier.

How to decide in one minute

You want your Tesla to look exactly as Tesla designed it, maybe protect only the front: ULTIMATE PLUS. You want a look almost nobody else has, you are ready to commit to the full car, and you like that it is reversible: STEALTH.
